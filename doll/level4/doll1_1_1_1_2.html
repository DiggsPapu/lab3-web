Story

Dorothy was in a corner hugging her broken doll.

She started to fell a warm hug from the doll.

Suddenly Dorothy heard a calm voice but she didn't get from where was it, the voice said:

Everything is okay now sweetheart don't worry, you are with me now.

At the beginning she felt like not everything was okay but the calm voice make her feel protected and not alone.


And soon she started to dream in the most pleasant dream.

Devil

The devil can be presented in a lot of ways, even though, at the beginning can seem pleasant we should be always aware that it can show up at any time.

In this world, is the destiny of mankind controlled by some transcendental entity or law? Is it like the hand of God hovering above? At least, it is true that man has no control, even over his own will.
