Story

Mollity heard a lot of barking, it was a pack of 12 or more dogs, they were searching for food.

Those dogs were aproaching to her safety and even though she wasn't a human, she started to feel something close to that feeling. If she'd have had a heart, it would have been beating really fast.

Any false move could mean, maybe not her death but something worse. Last time, she needed more than a few years to heal from an attack like it.

Those dogs were closer and her plastic heart was beating harder, she felt that her plastic heart could be heard all over the street.

One of those dogs started to rip the little box and she was so terrified of what could happen next...
